
# Norton Studios
## Provider of Digital Solutions for the Public Sector

Norton Studios is a trusted public sector delivery partner. We specialise Government Digital Services across the UK, with a particular focus on Accessibility (WCAG 2.1/2.2) and Responsive Website design.

We believe best practices matter, especially in the public sector. That's why we are vocal advocates for [Coding in the Open](https://www.gov.uk/service-manual/service-standard/point-12-make-new-source-code-open) and are practitioners of the [GDS Way](https://gds-way.digital.cabinet-office.gov.uk/standards/programming-languages.html#programming-languages).

Our services:

- Software Development
- Test Automation & Quality Assurance
- Architecture
- Design
- Agile Delivery
