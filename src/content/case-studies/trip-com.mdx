# Trip.com

Trip.com (formerly cTrip) is a Chinese travel agency that provides a one-stop travel booking service for flights, hotels, trains, car rentals, and more. The company was founded in 1999 and is headquartered in Shanghai, China. Trip.com is one of the largest online travel agencies in the world, with over 400 million users and 1.4 million hotels in 200 countries and regions.

## Case study

- Implementation of a new journey planner for the UK rail network.
- Implementation of a ticket issueing system for the UK rail network.
- RDG accreditation.
- Quality assurance - first of kind automated tooling to test the validity of journey planner against the National Routeing Guide.
