# Trip.com

Trip.com (formerly cTrip) is a Chinese travel agency that provides a one-stop travel booking service for flights, hotels, trains, car rentals, and more. The company was founded in 1999 and is headquartered in Shanghai, China. Trip.com is one of the largest online travel agencies in the world, with over 400 million users and 1.4 million hotels in 200 countries and regions.

Trip.com needed to enhance their service offerings for the UK rail network by implementing a new journey planner and ticket issuing system. They also required RDG accreditation and a robust quality assurance process to ensure the accuracy and reliability of their journey planner against the National Routeing Guide.

## Solution

Norton Studios provided a comprehensive solution to meet Trip.com's needs:

- **Journey Planner**: Developed a new journey planner for the UK rail network, ensuring seamless integration with existing systems.
- **Ticket Issuing System**: Implemented a ticket issuing system that streamlined the booking process for users.
- **RDG Accreditation**: Assisted Trip.com in obtaining RDG accreditation, ensuring compliance with industry standards.
- **Quality Assurance**: Created first-of-its-kind automated tooling to test the validity of the journey planner against the National Routeing Guide, ensuring high accuracy and reliability.

## Impact

The solutions provided by Norton Studios had a significant positive impact on Trip.com's operations:

- **Enhanced User Experience**: The new journey planner and ticket issuing system improved the overall user experience, making it easier for customers to book rail travel.
- **Compliance and Accreditation**: Achieving RDG accreditation ensured that Trip.com met industry standards, enhancing their credibility and trustworthiness.
- **Improved Accuracy**: The automated quality assurance tools ensured that the journey planner was highly accurate, reducing errors and increasing customer satisfaction.
- **Operational Efficiency**: The streamlined systems and processes led to increased operational efficiency, allowing Trip.com to better serve their customers and expand their market presence.
