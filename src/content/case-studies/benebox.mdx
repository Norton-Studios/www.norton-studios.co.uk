# Benebox

Benebox is a registered charity that provides an easy way for people to donate to a range of charities by saving their change when making purchases on e-commerce websites.

## Case study

Norton Studios provided a complete solution from delivery, design and development. The key challenge was finding an innovative solution to detect when users had completed a purchase and then prompt them to donate their change to a charity of their choice. Our solution was to create a Chrome extension and Next.js website.

### Key Technologies

- Next.js
- Typescript
